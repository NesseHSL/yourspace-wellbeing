The 30-day structure

Sofa to Studio is built around three progressive workouts — Foundation, Build, and Strengthen. You'll spend 10 active days with each one, following a 5-days-on, 2-days-off weekly rhythm. That rest is deliberate: recovery is when your body actually changes.

Phase 1 (Days 1–10): Foundation — Repeat this workout on your 5 active days each week.
Phase 2 (Days 11–20): Build — Move to this workout once you've completed Phase 1.
Phase 3 (Days 21–30): Strengthen — The final phase. You'll feel the difference.

A typical week looks like this:

Mon Workout
Tue Workout
Wed Workout
Thu Workout
Fri Workout
Sat Rest
Sun Rest

Progressing through the phases

Don't rush ahead. Each phase builds directly on the last — the muscle memory, alignment, and strength you develop in Foundation is the foundation (literally) for Build and Strengthen.

Each workout has beginner, intermediate, and advanced modifications built in. Start at whatever level feels honest for your body today, and progress when you can complete the session comfortably with good form.

By day 30, you'll be doing workouts that would have felt impossible on day one. That's the point.

Using the Macro Calculator

  • 1 Scroll down to the Nutrition section and open the Macro Calculator, or click the card above the calculator.
  • 2 Enter your weight (kg), height (cm), and age — then select your biological sex, activity level, and goal.
  • 3 Hit Calculate my macros. You'll see your daily calorie target and the protein, carbs, and fat breakdown to hit it.
  • 4 Use these numbers as a guide — not a rule. You may need to adjust slightly based on how you feel and how you perform in your sessions.

Using the Recipe Finder

  • 1 Click Recipe Finder in the Nutrition section to open the meal planner.
  • 2 Enter your calorie target (from the macro calculator) and any dietary preferences or restrictions.
  • 3 The planner will generate a high-protein meal plan to support your training days. Focus on protein — it protects muscle while you build strength.
