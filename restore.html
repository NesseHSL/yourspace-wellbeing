Get access — £24.99 See the programme

A note before you begin: Every postnatal body is different. We recommend getting clearance from your GP or midwife before starting any exercise programme after birth — typically at your 6-week check, or 8–12 weeks following a C-section. If you experience any pain, heaviness, or leaking during exercise, please stop and seek guidance from a women's health physiotherapist.

The Programme

Six sessions.
Eight weeks.

Restore is deliberately gradual. Each session is shorter than you might expect — because recovery isn't about effort, it's about consistency. We progress only when your body is ready.

1

Breathe & Reconnect

We begin with breath — because diaphragmatic breathing is the first step in restoring the relationship between your breath, your core, and your pelvic floor. This session is gentle, meditative, and more powerful than it looks. Everything starts here.

Diaphragmatic breathing Pelvic floor awareness Gentle mobility C-section safe
15 mins
Weeks 1–2
2

Pelvic Floor First

A dedicated session focused entirely on pelvic floor activation and progressive strengthening. We cover the full spectrum — activation, coordination, endurance, and relaxation — because a pelvic floor that can't let go is just as problematic as one that can't hold on.

Pelvic floor activation Coordination Endurance & release C-section safe
20 mins
Weeks 2–3
3

Core Reconnect

Now we introduce the deep core — specifically the transverse abdominis, which acts as your body's natural corset. Every exercise here is safe for diastasis recti and focused on reconnection rather than loading. No crunches. No sit-ups. Just intelligent, progressive core work.

Transverse abdominis Diastasis recti safe Deep core Spinal alignment
20 mins
Weeks 3–4
4

Posture Reset

Pregnancy, birth, and feeding all leave their mark on posture — rounded shoulders, tight hip flexors, a pelvis tipped forward or back. This session addresses those patterns directly, releasing what's tight and strengthening what's weak, so you can stand tall again.

Upper back Hip flexors Pelvic alignment Neck & shoulders
20 mins
Weeks 4–5
5

Rebuild Strength

With your foundations in place, we begin rebuilding functional strength — glutes, hips, and legs through progressive, supported movements. This session introduces load gradually, always with pelvic floor connection as the foundation. You'll feel strong — and you'll be surprised how much you've already rebuilt.

Glute strength Hip stability Functional movement Progressive load
25 mins
Weeks 5–7
6

Studio Ready

The final session brings everything together in a full-body workout that mirrors what you'll experience back at the studio — with the awareness, connection, and strength you've spent eight weeks building. This isn't just a workout. It's the moment you realise you're ready.

Full body Studio preparation Integration Confidence building
25 mins
Weeks 7–8
