Before we go any further

Nothing replaces the room.

There is a reason we built a studio before we built an app. When you move with other people — in the same space, breathing the same air, being seen by the same instructor — something different happens in your brain. Your mirror neurons fire. Your motivation lifts. Your effort increases, often without you even noticing. This is not a marketing claim. It is neuroscience.

"Studies show that exercising with others can increase pain tolerance, performance, and motivation — even when working at the same intensity as solo training."
Oxford University, 2009 — and replicated many times since.

The Home Studio is not a replacement for that. It is for the days when life gets in the way. When you travel. When the class is full. When you need to move but you cannot get to the studio — and you refuse to let the streak die.

Use it wisely. Use it regularly. And when you can, come and move with us in person.
